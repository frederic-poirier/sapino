Description

In traditional medicine, balsam fir gum is used to relieve inflammation and infection of the respiratory tract, making it an excellent natural remedy for colds and the flu. Additionally, balsam fir gum provides relief for those suffering from nervousness and anxiety, helping them sleep better.

Sapino balsam fir gum capsules are excellent for soothing throat irritation and clearing the lungs. This product is highly valued by smokers.

In addition to its natural benefits for the body, the liquid contained in the capsules (fir gum) can be used on cuts and bruises, acting as a healing and protective analgesic.

Sapino gums are composed exclusively of Canadian balsam fir.
