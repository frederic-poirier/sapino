Description

Sapino Balsam Fir with Echinacea lozenges are effective in relieving sore throats, nasal congestion, and coughs.

Traditionally used in herbal medicine to help fight and reduce the duration of upper respiratory infections.

In addition, Sapino lozenges contain natural menthol crystals to freshen your breath.

Sapino Fir & Echinacea lozenges are sugar-free (sweetened with sorbitol).

All of the products we offer have a product license issued by Health Canada, which you can find here: Health Products Canada

Medicinal ingredients (per 5 ml):

L-Menthol: 4.73 mg
Eucalyptus oil (Eucalyptus spp., leaf): 2.25 mg
Echinacea: 216 mg (43.2 mg Echinacea purpurea, root 5:1)

Non-medicinal ingredients:

Sorbitol, fir essential oil, magnesium stearate, chlorophyll.

Dosage (adults and children over 10 years):

Dissolve one lozenge slowly in the mouth as needed. Do not exceed 10 lozenges per day.

Duration of use:

For prolonged use, consult a healthcare professional.

Warning:

Consult a healthcare professional if symptoms worsen or persist for more than 2 days. Consult a healthcare professional prior to use if you are taking immunosuppressants, have an autoimmune disease, or have a progressive systemic disease such as tuberculosis, collagenosis, multiple sclerosis, AIDS, and/or HIV infection.

DO NOT TAKE IF THE INTEGRITY OF THE BAG IS COMPROMISED.
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
