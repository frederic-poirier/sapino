Discover the mini lozenges Fresh balsam fir lozenges made by Sapino. These refreshing lozenges are composed of a blend of balsam fir, eucalyptus, and menthol.

In addition to freshening your breath, Sapino mini lozenges, made with 100% natural ingredients, will help soothe your throat and relieve nasal congestion.

In traditional medicine, balsam fir gum is used to relieve inflammation and infection of the respiratory tract, making it an excellent natural remedy for colds and flu. Additionally, balsam fir gum provides relief for those suffering from nervousness and anxiety, helping them sleep better.

Sapino mini lozenges are made with 100% natural, Canadian ingredients.

Non-medicinal ingredients:

Sorbitol, fir essential oil, magnesium stearate, menthol crystals, eucalyptus oil, natural color (chlorophyll). NO ADDED SUGAR – SWEETENED WITH SORBITOL

Adult Dosage:
