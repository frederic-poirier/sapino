Description

Traditionally used in herbal medicine to help fight and reduce the duration of upper respiratory infections, extra-strength Balsam Fir lozenges relieve nasal congestion, coughs, and sore throats.

Made with natural menthol crystals, they freshen breath and allow you to breathe better.

Extra-strength Balsam Fir lozenges are sugar-free (sweetened with sorbitol).

All the products we offer have a product license issued by Health Canada, which you can find here: Health Products Canada

Ingredients Medicinal ingredients (per 5 ml):

L-Menthol: 4.73 mg
Eucalyptus oil (Eucalyptus spp, leaf): 2.25 mg

Non-medicinal ingredients:

Sorbitol, fir essential oil, magnesium stearate, chlorophyll.

Dosage (adults and children over 12 years):

Let one lozenge dissolve slowly in the mouth as needed. Do not exceed 10 lozenges per day.

Duration of use:

For prolonged use, consult a healthcare professional.

Caution:

Consult a healthcare professional if symptoms worsen or persist for more than 2 days or are accompanied by fever.
