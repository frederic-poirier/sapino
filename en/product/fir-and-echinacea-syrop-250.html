Description

Sapino's Fir & Echinacea Syrup is excellent for relieving coughs and nasal congestion.

The natural properties of Sapino Syrup are known to help prevent flu and cold symptoms by helping fight upper respiratory infections.

This syrup is alcohol-free.

All the products we offer have a product license issued by Health Canada, which you can find here: Health Products Canada

Medicinal ingredients (per 5 ml):

Echinacea root (Echinacea purpurea L. Moench): 43.75 mg (from a 4:1 extract)
Dried plant: 175 mg

Non-medicinal ingredients:

Canadian balsam fir needle essential oil, Citrus aurantium dulcis (sweet orange) fruit extract, fructose, glycerol, 5-hydroxydodecanoate, potassium sorbate, sodium benzoate, sorbitol, citric acid, Purified water, xanthan gum.

Adult dosage:

For adults (15 years and older): Take one teaspoon 6 times daily.
Children 5 to 14 years: Take one teaspoon 2 to 6 times daily.
Children 2 to 4 years: Take one teaspoon 1 to 4 times daily.

Directions for Use:

Take at the first sign of a cold or infection.

Duration of Use:

If symptoms persist or worsen, consult a healthcare professional.

Caution:

Consult a healthcare professional prior to use if you have a progressive systemic disease such as tuberculosis, collagenosis, multiple sclerosis, AIDS, and/or HIV infection, or if you have an autoimmune disease or are taking immunosuppressants.

DO NOT USE IF SAFETY SEAL IS BROKEN. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. SHAKE WELL BEFORE USE.
