Description

Sapino's balsam fir lozenges with thyme are effective in helping to relieve coughs. Traditionally used in herbal medicine as an expectorant to help relieve the symptoms of bronchitis and upper respiratory catarrh (anti-catarrh).

Fir Lozenges with Thyme have no added sugar (sweetened with sorbitol).

All the products we offer have a product license issued by Health Canada, which you can find here: Health Products Canada

Medicinal Ingredients (per 5 ml):

Thyme (Thymus vulgaris, Leaf, Flowering top (herb))…84 mg 5:1 extract, equivalent to 420 mg of dried herb.

Non-medicinal ingredients:

Sorbitol, Balsam Fir Needle Essential Oil, Oregano Leaf Oil, dl-Menthol, Eucalyptus Essential Oil, Chlorophyll.

Dosage (adults and children over 12 years):

1 lozenge 3 to 20 times daily. Allow one (1) lozenge to dissolve slowly in the mouth as needed. Do not swallow. Do not exceed 20 lozenges per day. Take at the first sign of a cold or infection.

Duration of use:

For prolonged use, consult a health professional.
