The power of fir gum

Fir gum and balsam fir essential oil offer interesting benefits. For centuries, Native Americans transformed fir gum into cough syrup and remedies to relieve injuries and illnesses. Thanks to its antibacterial, antiseptic, and calming effects, fir gum is used today to relieve coughs, sore throats, nasal congestion, colds, and the flu. Just like balsam fir essential oil, balsam fir gum can be used in your bath, as a cough syrup, or as a lozenge.
